Built-in Tools

Pedalboard3 includes several built-in processors for common tasks.

Audio Tools

Tuner

Chromatic tuner for guitars, bass, and other instruments. Displays note name, cents deviation, and frequency.

Tone Generator

Generate test tones (sine, saw, square, noise) for testing signal chains and tuner calibration. Supports frequency sweep and drift modes.

Oscilloscope

Real-time waveform display for visualizing audio signals.

VU Meter

Visual level metering for monitoring audio levels.

Metronome

Click track with tempo control and time signature settings.

Looper

Record and loop audio phrases for practice or performance.

Audio Recorder

Record audio to WAV files.

File Player

Play back audio files (WAV, MP3, etc.) through your signal chain.

Effects

IR Loader

Load impulse responses for cabinet simulation and reverb.

NAM (Neural Amp Modeler)

Load Neural Amp Modeler profiles for amp and pedal emulation.

Routing

Splitter / Mixer

Split signals into parallel paths or mix multiple signals together.

Level Control

Adjust gain and pan for any point in your signal chain.
